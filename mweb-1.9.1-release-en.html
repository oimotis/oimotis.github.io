Local Images Upload Services

Now you can upload local images to Google Photos, imgur, Custom API.

Just go to Perferences - Publishing to add Local Images Upload Services like below.

Click right-top corner share button - Upload Local Images to..:

Choose a image upload service and click Upload Images button, the local images will upload automatically!
